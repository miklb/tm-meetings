  8. File No. CRA25-16606

    Resolution of the Community Redevelopment Agency of the City of Tampa, Florida, approving the West Riverwalk Multi-Modal Network and Safety Improvements Grant Funding Agreement to construct a new multimodal network along West Tampa in connection with contract 22-C-00001; Tampa Multimodal Network and Safety Improvements Project by and between said Community Redevelopment Agency (“CRA”) and the City of Tampa, Florida (“Grant Recipient”) on property located within the boundaries of the West Tampa Community Redevelopment Area; authorizing execution of the Community Redevelopment Grant Funding Agreement by the Chair of the CRA and attestation by the CRA Secretary; providing an effective date. Memorandum from Brenda Thrower, CRA Development Manager, transmitting additional information for said agenda item.

    Background

    The project will complete the West Riverwalk within the West Tampa Community Redevelopment Area and will include, but not be limited to, the construction of a shared-use bicycle and pedestrian path along the western bank of the Hillsborough River from the center of West Cass Street to a point near Willow Avenue. At this northern riverfront end, the pathway will include an east-west connection to North Rome Avenue and will include pedestrian improvements through this corridor. The project will exclude a portion of the West Riverwalk path portion between the eastern right-of-way lines of both North Willow Avenue and North Oregon Avenue which portion is to be constructed by the Rome Yard Developer, Related Group, per their Master Development Agreement.

  11. File No. CRA25-17036

    Resolution of the Community Redevelopment Agency of the City of Tampa, Florida, approving the purchase of real property in the Channel District Community Redevelopment Area located at 1125 and 1127 east Twiggs Street, Tampa, Florida, for community redevelopment purposes at a purchase price of $4,500,000 together with closing costs in the approximate amount of $50,000, pursuant to a contract for sale and purchase between the City of Tampa, as Purchaser, and A & B Opportunities, Inc., a Florida corporation, as seller; authorizing payment of the purchase price and closing costs associated with the purchase of said parcel of real property with proceeds from the Channel District Land Assemblage Project within the Channel District CRA fund; providing an effective date. Memorandum from Courtney Orr, CRA Development Manager, transmitting additional information for said agenda item.

    Background

    Staff is seeking approval of expenditure of funds to purchase property at 1125 and 1127 E.

    Twiggs St. within the Channel District CRA. The intention of the property purchase will be for the purposes of expansion of Madison Street Park. A sales price of $4.5 million dollars was agreed upon with the seller, exceeding the property's appraised value of $2.9 million. As the property is currently being used for automotive purposes, it will require environmental site assessment and testing. Staff intends to set aside $500,000 from the sale in escrow post- closing for the purposes of completing remediation and to include a Brownfield designation.
