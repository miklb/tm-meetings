  8. File No. B2026-2

    Resolution levying an Ad Valorem Tax at the rate of six and two thousand, seventy-six ten thousandths (6.2076) mills upon all real and personal taxable property in the City of Tampa for the fiscal year 2026; Authorizing the Mayor and City Clerk, to certify such millage.

    Background

    This is a resolution levying an ad valorem tax of 6.2076 mills upon all real and personal taxable property in the City of Tampa for fiscal year 2026. This resolution must be approved by the governing body in order to levy millage per F.S. 200.065(2). It should be noted that the property tax yield of $380,255,500 represents 95% of taxable value as required under F.S.

    200.065(2).

  9. File No. B2026-2

    (UNAN) - An ordinance adopting the budget of the City of Tampa for the fiscal year beginning October 1, 2025 and ending September 30, 2026; Providing for the levy as provided by law of a tax on all taxable property in the City of Tampa and fixing the millage within said City; Making appropriations in accordance with the provisions of said budget; Authorizing and directing the Mayor and City Clerk, as the proper authorities of the City of Tampa to certify to the Property Appraiser of Hillsborough County, Florida, the millage to be levied for all purposes for the Fiscal Year 2026, in the City of Tampa; providing an effective date.

    Background

    This ordinance adopts the Fiscal Year 2026 annual budget in accordance with requirements set forth under Florida Statutes Chapter 200 and City of Tampa Charter Article VII. Finances.

    It should be noted that the property tax yield of $380,255,500 represents 95% of taxable value as required under F.S. 200.065(2).

  10. File No. B2026-2

    Resolution adopting the City of Tampa Capital Improvement Budget and Program for FY2026 through FY2030.

    Background

    The City's FY2026 Capital Improvement Program contains capital improvement projects totaling $422,138,440. The City's FY2026 - FY2030 Capital Improvement Program contains capital improvement projects totaling $1,719,951,757. This resolution adopts the FY2026 through FY2030 Recommended Capital Improvement Budget and Program as required by Florida Statutes and City Charter. In addition, it adopts the FY2026 portion of the FY2026 through FY2030 Capital Improvement Projects program as part of the FY2026 Annual Budget in the amount of $422,138,440.
