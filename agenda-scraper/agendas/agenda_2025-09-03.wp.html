    Background

    This ordinance adopts the Fiscal Year 2026 annual budget in accordance with requirements set forth under Florida Statutes Chapter 200 and City of Tampa Charter Article VII. Finances.

    It should be noted that the property tax yield of $380,255,500 represents 95% of taxable value as required under F.S. 200.065(2).
