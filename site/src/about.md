# About Tampa Meetings

Tampa Meetings is a civic transparency project by [The Tampa Monitor](https://tampamonitor.com) that makes Tampa City Council meeting records more accessible.

## What's here

- **Agendas** — Structured data extracted from the city's [Hyland OnBase](https://tampagov.hylandcloud.com/221agendaonline/) system, with links to supporting documents.
- **Transcripts** — Transcripts sourced from the city's [Closed Captioning Meeting Transcripts](https://apps.tampagov.net/cttv_cc_webapp/) , converted to readable sentence case.
- **Video** — Recordings from the [City of Tampa Meetings](https://www.youtube.com/@cityoftampameetings) YouTube channel, synced to transcript timestamps so you can click any line to jump to that moment.

## Meeting types

- **City Council** — Regular weekly session, typically Thursday mornings.
- **CRA** — Community Redevelopment Agency board meeting, same council members acting as the CRA board.
- **Evening Session** — Land use and zoning hearings held Thursday evenings.
- **Workshop** — Discussion sessions on specific topics, no formal votes.
- **Special Call** — as defined by council.

## Data sources

All data is sourced from official City of Tampa records. This is an unofficial resource and is not affiliated with the City of Tampa.

Document links point to mirrored copies for long-term stability. For original documents, refer to the [official agenda in OnBase](https://tampagov.hylandcloud.com/221agendaonline/).
